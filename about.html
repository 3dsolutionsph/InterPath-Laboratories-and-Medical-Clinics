Our Company

Interpath Laboratories and Medical Clinics is owned and managed by InterPath Laboratories Inc. A corporation determined to upgrade the laboratory services in the Calabarzon region and in the country. The corporation are managed by a group of allied medical professionals who have years of experience in providing quality health care.
Interpath Laboratories and Medical Clinics was inaugurated last September 8, 2013. Its pilot branch is located at 2nd Floor MORE Business Center, Ortigas Avenue Extension, San Isisdro, Taytay, Rizal. Interpath Laboratories and Medical Clinics initially offers complete laboratory exams, ECG and a multispecialty clinics (Pedia, OB-Gyne, Internal Medicine, Family Medicine, General Surgery and Cardiology). After a year in the market, additional diagnostic services were offered such as X-ray, 2d Echocardiogram with Doppler and Ultrasound Examination.

Quality is ingrained in our daily practice. We maintain rigorous internal and external quality control based on established specifications. Our skilled medical team delivers precise diagnostics, ensuring accurate, reliable, and timely results.

Patient-Centered Care and Compassion

We are dedicated to providing exceptional patient care and amenities, treating every patient with warmth, professionalism, and respect. We emphasize a personal touch, ensuring you feel valued and cared for through clear communication and a focus on your comfort and convenience.

Trust, Professionalism, and Expertise

You can trust us as a reliable medical diagnostics provider. Managed by experienced allied medical professionals, our highly qualified team possesses advanced training to deliver expert diagnostic services and analysis.
